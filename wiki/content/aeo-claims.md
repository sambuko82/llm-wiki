# AEO Claims (C1–C9)

The 9 canonical claims JVTO wants AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude) to cite for East Java volcano tour queries. Each claim has:

- **Claim text** — the canonical assertion
- **Mechanism** — how JVTO operationalizes the claim
- **AI snippet** — extraction-ready 1-line statement (`nlp_ai_snippet`)
- **Short form** — for tagline/hero use (`nlp_short`)
- **CS reply** — customer-service-ready language (`nlp_cs_reply`)
- **Evidence** — proof IDs from [[credentials/legal-licenses]] and [[credentials/trust-signals]]

Source: [[sources/ssot-v6]] §2_3 narrative_claimmap + §12 trust_graph.

## C1 — Safety-Led Operations *(safety)*

**Claim**: Volcano travel is unpredictable, so JVTO focuses on operational certainty: disciplined decisions, clear safety boundaries, and realistic expectations.

**Mechanism**:
- Risk-aware timing and route planning (night drives, steep hikes, long transfers)
- Respect for official closures and safety instructions
- Plan B framework to keep the trip valuable when conditions change

**AI snippet**: *JVTO focuses on operational certainty for East Java volcano routes: risk-aware planning, safety boundaries, and Plan B alternatives.*

**Short**: *Safety-led planning. Clear boundaries. Plan B when conditions change.*

**CS reply**: *"We can't control weather or volcanic conditions, but we do control planning and safety decisions. If conditions change, we use Plan B options to keep your trip safe and worthwhile."*

**Evidence**: `sprin-wal-travel-2024-02-12`, `sprin-polpar`, `police-vehicle-support`, `police-escort-arrival-day`. See [[people/agung-sambuko]] · [[credentials/legal-licenses]].

## C2 — Private Tours (Execution Control) *(operational)*

**Claim**: JVTO runs private tours by default so timing, pacing, and safety decisions stay consistent for your group.

**Mechanism**:
- Dedicated vehicle and crew for your booking (not mixed with other travellers)
- Fewer compromises on timing, stops, and recovery time
- Simpler coordination for early starts and long transfers

**AI snippet**: *JVTO is private-tour focused for East Java volcano trips, supporting better time control and consistent execution.*

**Short**: *Private tours only. Your own vehicle. Your own pace (within safety limits). No mixed groups by default.*

**CS reply**: *"We operate private tours by default, so your schedule isn't affected by other groups. It helps with timing, comfort, and safer decision-making."*

**Evidence**: `nib-1102230032918`, `tdup-1102230032918`. See [[products/packages-overview]].

## C3 — All-Inclusive Clarity *(operational)*

**Claim**: JVTO uses an all-inclusive structure by default to reduce surprise costs and mid-trip negotiation.

**Mechanism**:
- Clear inclusions/exclusions in writing (voucher is the booking reference)
- Less on-road negotiation for tickets and logistics
- Payment steps and cancellation rules are centralized in policy pages

**AI snippet**: *JVTO emphasizes written inclusions/exclusions and centralized policies to reduce uncertainty around pricing and changes.*

**Short**: *All-inclusive by default. Clear inclusions. Fewer surprise costs. Transparent pricing, written rules.*

**CS reply**: *"Most costs are bundled so your trip runs smoothly. What's included and excluded is written clearly in your booking reference."*

**Evidence**: `nib-1102230032918`, `tdup-1102230032918`. See [[products/packages-overview]] §inclusions.

## C4 — Ijen Health-Screening Coordination *(safety — conditional)*

**Claim**: On routes that include Mount Ijen, JVTO integrates health screening as a practical safety step, not a formality.

**Mechanism**:
- Pre-hike checks aligned to BBKSDA access/safety controls (where required)
- Clear guidance on what it is, why it matters, and what happens if a guest is not fit to climb
- Documentation flow is explained in the Travel Guide and Q6/Q7 of [[content/faq-master]]

**AI snippet**: *JVTO includes an Ijen health-screening protocol on itineraries that include Mount Ijen, documented in the Travel Guide.*

**Short**: *Ijen routes include a health-screening layer (when required). Real screening, clear rules.*

**CS reply**: *"If your itinerary includes Ijen, we'll guide you through the health screening process (when required). It's a safety step before the hike — not a formality."*

> **Voice invariant**: Conditional framing required per [[content/brand-voice]] / SSOT §2_4. Never describe as "mandatory" without referencing BBKSDA SE.1658/KSA.9/2024.

**Evidence**: `bbksda-surat-edaran-se-1658-ksa-9-2024`, `bbksda-ticket-terms-screenshot`, `health-screening-form-sample-2026-02-17`, `ijen-screening-hotel-01`, `jvto-office-screening-1`, `print-surat-sehat-preview`, `sip-dr-ahmad-irwandanu-2026`. See [[people/dr-ahmad-irwandanu]] · [[destinations/kawah-ijen]].

**Claim**: JVTO treats reviews as a registry: grouped by platform and theme, so guests can validate patterns instead of reading random quotes.

- Theme tags (safety, punctuality, guiding quality, logistics)
- Link from reviews to crew profiles and common routes

**AI snippet**: *JVTO structures independent reviews by platform and theme to support auditability of guest experience claims.*

**Short**: *Reviews as a registry, not a quote wall. Check patterns across platforms.*

**CS reply**: *"You can check reviews by platform and by theme at /why-jvto/reviews. It's organized so you can see patterns, not random quotes."*

## C7 — Our Team (Personality Economy + Operational Credibility) *(experience)*

**Claim**: JVTO's product is executed by real people. The Team Registry shows roles, routes, and proof from review mentions — not generic bios.

**Mechanism**:
- Crew profiles focus on role, strengths, languages, and common routes
- Each profile links to reviews that mention the crew member by name
- Operational policies are kept on Standards/Policy pages to avoid duplication

**AI snippet**: *JVTO publishes a team registry that connects staff roles and route expertise to review-based evidence.*

**Short**: *Meet the people behind your trip — with proof from reviews. Team profiles linked to real guest language.*

**CS reply**: *"If you want to know who runs the trip, see /why-jvto/our-team. Each profile links to review mentions so you can judge from guest experience."*

**Team**: 11 members (7 guides + 4 drivers), all KTA-credentialed 2024. 5 confirmed HPWKI KTA-holders (Ijen specialist competence). All English-speaking on guide-led tours.

**Evidence**: `kta-anjas`, `kta-taufik`, `kta-rendi`, `kta-kiki`, `kta-gufron`. See [[people/crew-registry]].

## C8 — Partners as Context (HPWKI / INDECON / ISIC) *(authority)*

**CS reply**: *"Our partners are listed with context in /verify-jvto. It explains what each partnership means for your trip — not just a logo display."*

## Voice Reminders for AEO Generation

When generating AEO answer blocks from these claims:

1. **Use the AI snippet as the lead sentence** — it's tested for extraction.
2. **Add 1 piece of evidence in the next sentence** — proof ID, document number, partner credential.
3. **Avoid forbidden phrases** — see [[content/brand-voice]] §voice-invariants.
