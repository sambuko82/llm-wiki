# JVTO Operational Facts

*Practical, citable facts from the live knowledge base ([[sources/db-export-2026-05]]). Use in AEO answers, FAQ copy, and chatbot responses.*

---

## Temperatures

| Destination | Temperature range | When |
|---|---|---|
| Mount Bromo | 5–15°C (41–59°F) | Pre-dawn sunrise slot |
| Kawah Ijen | 10–15°C (50–59°F) | Midnight departure + crater rim |

Both destinations require warm layers — layering recommended over single thick garment. See [[sources/jvto-travel-guide-en]] §6 packing guidance.

## Arrival & Departure Timing Guidelines

- **Day 1 latest pick-up**: 16:00 WIB (4:00 PM) — later pick-ups compress transfer time on the first night
- **Last-day flight recommendation**: depart no earlier than 18:00 WIB (6:00 PM); 20:00 WIB is safer for contingencies
- These guidelines apply to all packages starting or ending in Surabaya

## Ijen Closure Schedule

- **Regular closure**: First Friday of every month — Ijen crater is closed to visitors
- **Additional closures**: issued by BBKSDA East Java based on volcanic gas levels and safety assessments
- JVTO follows official BBKSDA closure announcements; no bypass of restrictions
- For scheduled closure dates, check bbksdajatim.org

---

## Ijen Practical Facts

- **Trolley ojek**: A man-powered cart/porter service is available at extra cost for guests who need assistance on the ascent or descent — not a JVTO inclusion, arranged on-site
- **Health screening location**: Nurse visits guest's hotel the evening before the hike (Bondowoso / Banyuwangi area)
- **Trek difficulty**: Moderate — ~1.5–2 hours each way; steep rocky trail
- **Gas mask**: Provided by JVTO as standard inclusion on all Ijen-relevant packages
- **No Valid QR Code = No Crater Zone Access** (per BBKSDA SE.1658/KSA.9/2024)

---

## Bromo Practical Facts

- **Milky Way stargazing**: Available on overnight packages departing at ~02:00 WIB (weather permitting)
- **Horse riding at crater**: Optional, extra cost, arranged on-site (not a JVTO inclusion)
- **Luggage during sunrise**: Main luggage stays at hotel; guests carry only a daypack for the jeep sequence
- **Yadnya Kasada festival**: Annual Tengger Hindu ceremony at Pura Luhur Poten (caldera floor) — date varies (lunar calendar). Bromo access may be restricted or heavily crowded. JVTO activates Plan-B SOP for bookings during this period.

---

## Waterfall Facts

**Tumpak Sewu:**
- Steep, wet trail with fixed ropes and handholds on canyon descent
- Water shoes strongly recommended (grip on river rocks)
- Helmets provided — see `> Contradiction` note in packages-overview (helmets are local site management provision, not JVTO-supplied per SSOT canonical)

**Madakaripura:**
- Canyon approach: wading through stream, narrow passages, wet stones
- You WILL get wet — waterproof bags and a change of clothes essential
- Helmets provided by local site management (NOT a JVTO inclusion per [[products/packages-overview]] §madakaripura-caveat)

## Tour Customization Policy ("Micro-customization")

JVTO allows micro-customizations that don't alter the primary route or duration:
- ✅ Twin beds instead of double bed
- ✅ Hotel room category upgrade (may cost extra)
- ✅ Adding optional on-site activities (horse riding at Bromo, trolley ojek at Ijen)
- ✅ Adjusting meal plans within operational limits
- ❌ Unbundled / transport-only services — not offered (end-to-end quality control policy)
- ❌ Significant route changes that alter primary destination sequence

## Dietary Accommodation

JVTO accommodates: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free diets. Must be declared at booking — cannot be confirmed on arrival. Source: [[sources/db-export-2026-05]] `knowledge_bases.special-dietary-needs`.

## Money Exchange Guidance

Downtown money changers have better rates than airports. JVTO does not earn referral from any money changer and is not responsible for exchange rates. Practical note to share during pre-trip briefing.

---

## Partial Tours / Transport-Only

Not offered. JVTO policy: no unbundled transport-only or partially customized services. End-to-end quality control is the operational model. Guests seeking "driver only" or "car rental" should use a different provider. Source: [[sources/db-export-2026-05]] `knowledge_bases.partial-tours-transport-only`.
