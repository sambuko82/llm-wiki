### 2. SE Full Reference

**SE.1658/K2/BIDTEK.1/KSA/9/2024** — more complete reference number for the Surat Edaran governing TWA Kawah Ijen SOPs. Existing wiki uses shorter form SE.1658/KSA.9/2024. Both refer to same document.

### 3. RKW Jurisdictions

Kawah Ijen falls under **RKW 18** and **RKW 15** (Resor Konservasi Wilayah) of BBKSDA Jawa Timur.

### 4. "Lamborghini Ijen" Trolley Evacuation

The traditional mining trolley repurposed for medical evacuation has the nickname "Lamborghini Ijen." Evacuation protocol: **3 personnel to pull uphill, 2 personnel to stabilize downhill.**

### 5. Sengkan Gandrung — High-Risk Braking Zone

The steep descent road from Ijen toward Banyuwangi (Sengkan Gandrung area) is classified as a high-risk braking zone. Two motorcycle accidents (April 2025) occurred here. JVTO requires professional driver certification for transit through this zone.

Corroborated by [[sources/ijen-tourist-accidents]] — Saxena Akansa (India) and Piere Ghirardy Johan (France) incidents.

### 6. TWA Kawah Ijen Call Center

Emergency contact: **0812 2018 0930**. Used as initial report point in SAR incidents.

### 7. SAR Protocol (from Muhammad Dzikri Maulana 2026 case)

1. Initial report → TWA Call Center
2. SRU (Search and Rescue Units) formed: BKSDA, TNI, Polri, HPWKI
3. Hasty Search of 3.6km trail
4. Type II Open Grid search — 1km² area around last known position
5. Thermal drone deployment for cliff-side signatures
6. Tactical transport via trolley → Puskesmas Licin

### 8. Risk Flag System

| Flag | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Green** | Normal/Safe |
| **Yellow** | Caution — alert for gas or trail instability |
| **Red** | High Risk — masks mandatory due to gas concentration; area closures may be imminent |

### 9. Ijen Rijik Environmental Detail

Monthly cleanup removes **100–150 kg waste** from Paltuding to crater rim. Coordinated by BKSDA, SAR, and HPWKI. Includes targeted planting of endemic **Cemara Gunung** (*Casuarina junghuhniana*) to stabilize trail slopes and prevent erosion.

### 10. Mandatory Guide Behaviors

1. "Green Briefings" — safety and environmental orientations pre-ascent
2. 500m Hazard Exclusion Zone from crater floor (PVMBG recommended)
3. Navigational discipline — absolute group cohesion
4. Authority integration — coordination with Pam Obvit under SPRIN framework
