## Three Policies Covered

### Policy 1 — Booking, Payment & Cancellation

**Key facts:**
- Official channels: `javavolcano-touroperator.com` / WhatsApp `+62 822-4478-8833` / `hello@javavolcano-touroperator.com`
- Booking confirmed only when: (1) package selected, (2) required payment processed, (3) Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) issued
- Currency: **IDR only** (official pricing and accounting)
- Standard deposit: **20%** of total booking value
- If Day 1 is within **14 days**: up to **100% full payment** may be required
- Balance payment deadline by card: **no later than 5 days before Day 1**
- Balance payment deadline by bank transfer / Wise: **no later than 3 days before Day 1**
- Cash at JVTO office (IDR only): only if approved in writing

**Bank transfer details (balance payments only):**
- Bank BRI — PT Java Volcano Rendezvous — 001301001779564 — SWIFT: BRINIDJAXXX
- Bank BCA — PT Java Volcano Rendezvous — 1200944352 — SWIFT: CENAIDJAXXX

**Payment security:**
- SSL-encrypted checkout, PCI DSS-compliant gateway
- JVTO never asks for full card numbers, CVV, or banking credentials via chat or email

**Amendments:** One free reschedule (≥48h before Day 1), subject to availability. <48h not permitted.

**Cancellation (48-hour cut-off, local Indonesia time):**
- ≥48h before Day 1: 100% converts to JVTO Travel Credit (IDR, non-expiring, transferable with written confirmation)
- <48h before Day 1: booking forfeited up to 100%; Travel Credit and cash refunds generally not provided

**Force majeure:** JVTO may cancel/postpone/reroute for volcanic activity, gas, earthquakes, floods, government closures, civil unrest, severe weather. May offer: reasonable alternatives, Travel Credit for unused portion, partial/full cash refund net of non-recoverable expenses.

**Health / Ijen:** If guest denied clearance, affected components are non-refundable (costs pre-committed).

**FOC scheme:**
- 18 paying pax → 1 FOC
- 35 paying pax → 2 FOC
- 50 paying pax → 3 FOC + **5% discount for entire group** ("pax" = paying guests only)

**Document precedence:**
1. Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)
2. Booking, Payment & Cancellation Policy
3. Inclusions & Exclusions Policy
4. Privacy & Data Protection Policy
5. Travel Guide — Booking Information

### Policy 2 — Inclusions & Exclusions

**Write-it-to-bind-it rule:** Only inclusions on the official tour page AND Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) are legally binding.

**Standard inclusions:**
- Private air-conditioned vehicle for all land sectors in confirmed itinerary (fuel, tolls, parking)
- Vehicle allocation by group size:
  - 2–3 guests: 1 × MPV (e.g., Toyota Avanza or similar)
  - 4–9 guests: 1 × Toyota Hiace (or similar 16-seat minibus)
  - 10–11 guests: 1 × Toyota Hiace + 1 × MPV (for seating and luggage space)
- Bromo jeep (when Bromo included): private 4WD jeep, **max ±4 guests per jeep** for comfort and safety
- Accommodation (hotel/homestay) for all overnight stays in itinerary
- Room allocation: 1 room per 2 guests; odd groups get extra bed; max 2 guests per standard room
- Professional crew adjusted by group size:
  - 2–3 guests: 1 × driver-guide (English-speaking) + licensed local site guides
  - 4–11 guests: 1 × professional driver + 1 × escort guide + licensed local site guides
- Entrance tickets and park permits for all confirmed itinerary sites
- Ijen safety gear (when Ijen included): professional gas mask, trekking poles, mandatory medical check + digital/printed clearance
- Daily bottled mineral water in vehicle for overland sectors
- Hotel breakfasts for each included overnight
- Java-Bali ferry tickets (when crossing is listed in itinerary and ticket specified as included)
- 1 × JVTO travel T-shirt per participant for eligible packages
- Custom JVTO group T-shirt for groups ≥12 guests in a single booking (when written on voucher)

**Conditional inclusions (only if written on voucher):**
- Custom group T-shirts
- Additional hosted meals (dinner before Ijen; lunch after Tumpak Sewu; special dinners)
- Bali hotel/airport transfers beyond standard ferry crossing
- Early check-in / late check-out
- Dedicated photographer/videographer
- Police escort or special convoy for VIP/large groups (subject to availability and regulation)

**Exclusions (default unless written):**
- International/domestic flights, long-distance trains, visas
- Travel insurance
- Personal expenses (snacks, drinks, souvenirs, laundry)
- Tips/gratuities

### Policy 3 — Privacy & Data Protection

**Data collected:**
- Contact and booking details (name, email, WhatsApp, booking refs)
- Participant details (guest list, passport/ID where required for permits, pickup/rooming preferences)
- Safety/logistics info (dietary needs, relevant health info where necessary)

**Data use:** E-Voucher delivery, logistics coordination, customer support, legal/permit compliance, safety/emergency coordination.

**Data sharing:** Only with accommodation partners, crew, ticketing/permit partners, medical screening providers — minimum necessary for operational purposes. No commercial sharing.

**Payment:** Cards processed via secure gateway. JVTO does not store full card numbers, CVV, or online banking passwords.
