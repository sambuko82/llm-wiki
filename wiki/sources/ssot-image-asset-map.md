## Notable Facts for Content Production

1. **Police presence is visually documented**: 8 field operations images include 2 police escort photos (day + night), 1 police vehicle, 1 Geopark briefing, and 1 Baratha Hotel departure lineup — all showing actual Polri presence, not uniforms in a studio.
2. **Booking.com 2015 shipping label is a continuity anchor**: Address on the box ("Agung, Jl. Khairil Anwar No.102") is the same as today's PT office. Two images prove it (plaque + box label).
3. **Screening has 5 photos**: Two locations (hotel + office), multiple procedures (blood pressure, vitals, SpO2), plus the surat sehat printout — enough for a full "How Ijen Health Screening Works" visual section.
4. **All 11 portrait crew have a live photo URL**: Ready to use directly in content without additional uploads. Fauzi, Boy, Joyo, and Holili are in portraits only (no KTA card image published).
