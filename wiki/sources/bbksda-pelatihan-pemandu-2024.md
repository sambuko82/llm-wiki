# Source: BBKSDA Jatim — Pelatihan Pemandu Kawah Ijen

## Provenance

Official institutional report by BBKSDA Jawa Timur (Balai Besar Konservasi Sumber Daya Alam Jawa Timur) — the government nature conservation agency responsible for Kawah Ijen Natural Tourism Park. Published 2024-05-24. Not JVTO-authored. SHA-256: `press-bbksda-ijen-guide-training` (see [[credentials/legal-licenses]] §sha256-anchors).

## Key Facts

**Training program**: Three-day event, May 20–22, 2024. Location: Paltuding (the Ijen trailhead and visitor centre inside the park).

**Host**: BBKSDA Jawa Timur, coordinated by Section V KSDA.

**Participants**: Targeted 250 HPWKI-affiliated guides (Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen). Local guides outside HPWKI also attended.

**Curriculum topics**:
1. Conservation area awareness
2. Local guide responsibilities within Kawah Ijen Natural Tourism Park
3. SAR (Search and Rescue) techniques
4. PPGD — Penanggulangan Penderita Gawat Darurat (Emergency Medical Response / First Aid for emergency casualties)

**Program purpose**: Enhance guide capacity, prepare guides to respond to visitor accidents, and position guides as field extensions of park management. Recurring sessions planned as forums for technique refreshment and policy communication.
