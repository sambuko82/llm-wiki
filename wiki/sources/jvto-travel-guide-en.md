## Key Facts (verbatim from source)

### Booking Process (5 steps)
1. Choose route/package, select date, click Instant Book
2. Enter lead traveler email + WhatsApp
3. Review & pay via secure checkout
4. JVTO issues Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)
5. Access My Booking Portal

### My Booking Portal
Post-payment access using Lead Traveler email. Shows: tour dates, package summary, pickup details, vehicle/crew assignment, accommodation plan, daily itinerary, notes, E-Voucher download, To-Do checklist (guest list, pickup/drop-off, rooming, dietary notes).

### Ijen Health Screening — Digital QR System
- Results stored in a digital system; QR code allows checkpoint staff to verify real screening was done
- Purpose: reduce paper forgeries, improve consistency, keep more visitors within safe limits
- Available to non-JVTO travelers via participating clinics/hotels in Ijen area (not a competitive restriction — raises overall safety standard)
- Screening does NOT guarantee nothing can go wrong; does NOT replace personal doctor advice
- Conditions on mountain can still change — JVTO may still cancel hike after screening

### Packing — Destination-Specific

**Bromo:**
- Warm layers (5–10°C at viewpoints early morning)
- Hat, gloves, scarf/buff
- Comfortable shoes (sand and dust terrain)
- Fitness: basic mobility — step in/out of jeep, walk short distances on uneven ground

**Ijen:**
- Warm layers for night start
- Long sleeves and trousers
- Good hiking shoes with grip (dusty or slippery trail)
- Headlamp (often provided by JVTO but bring your own as backup)
- Spare mask/scarf for dust (NOT a replacement for JVTO-provided gas mask at crater area)
- **Silver jewelry warning**: Sulfur gases tarnish silver — DO NOT wear silver rings, bracelets, necklaces, earrings, or silver-coated watches during Ijen activity. May be difficult/impossible to reverse.
- Fitness: sustained uphill walk 1–2 hours at steady pace; guests with heart/lung/circulation conditions must consult doctor

**Tumpak Sewu:**
- Clothing and shoes that can get wet and muddy
- Sandals or water shoes with grip for river sections
- Small towel and change of clothes
- Fitness: comfortable with balance, descending/ascending with ropes/handholds, slippery ground; NOT recommended for serious knee, ankle, or balance issues

### Safety Decision Framework (JVTO)
Before tour: check road/park conditions, weather alerts, hotel/transport availability, align with local partners.
During tour: monitor weather/road/crowding/local announcements; may leave earlier/later, switch viewpoints, choose safer paths.
If plans change: explain why, propose reasonable alternatives, apply Travel Credit if key elements cannot operate.
Alcohol/substances: guests under influence may be refused certain activities (hikes, steep trails).

### Weather Patterns
Bromo & Ijen main activities happen between late night and early morning — before heavier rain common later in day. Weather never fully predictable; JVTO schedules to maximise chance of safe/enjoyable conditions. Official Indonesian volcanic alert sources followed; JVTO will NOT bypass authority restrictions.

### Travel Credit (confirmed from Travel Guide)
- JVTO uses Travel Credit instead of cash refunds
- Non-expiring, transferable with written authorization
- No cash refunds except where required by applicable law
- 48h cut-off (local Indonesia time):
  - ≥48h before Day 1: 100% → Travel Credit
  - <48h before Day 1: up to 100% forfeited; generally no Travel Credit

### Police Escort (Travel Guide — confirmed details)
- JVTO does NOT provide escort vehicles itself
- Formal request submitted to competent Traffic Police unit
- Approved/declined per regulations, availability, and route definitions
- When approved: uniformed traffic police in official vehicles, written orders
- Not automatic; not a guarantee of special treatment; does not bypass speed limits or road rules
- Costs clearly listed in program/invoice; no unofficial on-road payments requested
- For large groups: inform JVTO of group size, vehicle count, route for feasibility assessment
