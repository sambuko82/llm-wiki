# Detik.com — "Suka Duka Polisi Pariwisata Bondowoso" (March 2021)

**Publication**: Detik.com — Berita Jawa Timur (national Indonesian news outlet)
**Author**: Chuk Shatu Widarsha
**Published**: 2021-03-14
**Source**: Obsidian Web Clipper — `Clippings/Suka Duka Polisi Pariwisata Bondowoso Tegakkan Prokes Sambil Lawan Dingin.md`

> Note: The clipped file contains significant navigation/sidebar noise from full-page capture. Core article content is approximately 8 paragraphs (lines 93–121 of the clipping file).

## Key Facts

- **Confirms by name**: **Bripka Agung Sambuko** as active Polisi Pariwisata (Tourist Police) — Polres Bondowoso
- **Location**: Kawah Wurung and surrounding Ijen highland plateau (Bondowoso jurisdiction)
- **Context**: COVID-19 pandemic long-holiday weekend — Polpar enforcing health protocols (prokes) at tourist sites
- **Duration of deployment**: Bripka Agung Sambuko stayed continuously on-site, did not return home, for multiple days starting Thursday
- **Conditions**: Temperature at Kawah Wurung reached 10°C; rain and fog — physically demanding deployment

## Direct Quotes (verbatim, Bahasa Indonesia)

> "Sejak libur hari Kamis kemarin, saya tak pernah pulang. Stand by dan nginap di sini terus." — **Bripka Agung Sambuko**, Detik.com 2021-03-14

*Translation: "Since the Thursday holiday, I haven't gone home at all. Standing by and sleeping here the whole time."*

> "Ya mau gimana lagi, Mas. Sudah tugas. Yang penting, masyarakat yang berwisata aman dari ancaman di situasi pandemi ini. Dengan tetap menerapkan prokes." — **Bripka Agung Sambuko**

*Translation: "What can I do, it's just duty. The important thing is that tourists are safe from threats in this pandemic situation, while still enforcing health protocols."*

## What This Source Proves

1. **Identity confirmation**: Detik.com (independent national media) names "Bripka Agung Sambuko" as a Polisi Pariwisata officer at Bondowoso — no operator self-attestation involved.
2. **Active field duty**: Quote confirms overnight deployment at the Ijen highland area — not an administrative or desk role.
3. **Tourist safety orientation**: His stated motivation — "yang penting, masyarakat yang berwisata aman" — directly maps to JVTO's safety-led founding narrative.
4. **Unit / jurisdiction**: Polisi Pariwisata Bondowoso — specialized unit under Polres Bondowoso, assigned to tourist objects. Trained in tourism and language skills (article confirms English + other language training for Polpar officers).
5. **Temporal context**: March 2021. JVTO PT was operational (incorporated 2016). This article pre-dates TDUP formalization (2023) but confirms active police status during the JVTO operational period.
6. **Polpar unit characteristics** (from article): officers trained in English and other languages; wear police uniform with maroon accents; stationed permanently at tourist objects during peak periods.
