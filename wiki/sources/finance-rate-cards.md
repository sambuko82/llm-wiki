# Finance Rate Cards

*Source summary for `raw/FINANCE/rate_cards/` — 5 JSON files containing JVTO internal cost components.*

**Files:**
- `Copy of crew-roles-rate-card.json` — 5 roles (Escort Guide Senior/Junior, Driver cum Guide, Escort Guide, Driver Only)
- `Copy of vehicle-rate-card.json` — 6 vehicle types (MPV, Innova, Hiace, Hiace Premio, Mini Bus Elf, Medium Bus)
- `Copy of accommodation-rate-card.json` — 23 hotels, 100+ room configurations across 7 locations
- `Copy of activities-rate-card.json` — 27 activity cost items across 6 destination groups
- `Copy of other-activities-rate-card.json` — 10 operational cost items

**Key facts:**
- All prices in IDR
- Crew rates: 200,000–275,000/day
- Vehicle rates: 250,000–1,800,000/day
- Police Escort is the highest single item: 2,000,000/engagement
- Accommodation spans 150,000 (Doho Superior) to 5,200,000 (Shanaya Royal Villa)
- Some hotels have weekday/weekend/high-season tiered pricing (Lava View Lodge, Manis Ae, THE 1O1, Shanaya)
- Activities vendor-tagged where applicable (Widhi Bromo for Bromo tickets and jeeps)
- Medical Checkup (35,000/pax) and Gas Mask (55,000/pax) are Ijen-specific safety costs
