## Accommodation Rules (from packages-overview + policy pack)

- **Room allocation**: 1 room per 2 guests (King or Twin) by default
- **Odd groups**: 1 room per 2 guests + 1 extra bed in one room
- **Max 2 guests per standard room** (excluding designated extra bed)
- **Couple preference**: King bed on request
- **Twin beds**: for non-couple room sharing on request
- **Room upgrade** (extra single room instead of extra bed): subject to availability, cost confirmed on voucher

---

## Hotel Amenity Context

Hotels are matched to the itinerary phase:
- **Bondowoso/Banyuwangi**: position guests for midnight Ijen departure + post-hike recovery
- **Bromo area**: position for 01:00–02:00 departure for Penanjakan viewpoint
- **Surabaya city finish**: provides clean airport transit with breakfast on departure day

*JVTO manages hotel bookings on guests' behalf — guests do not book independently. Hotel name on voucher is binding.*

## Trust Anchors

Hotel quality supports **C5** (proof-first transparency — named hotels on voucher) and **C3** (all-inclusive clarity — accommodation with breakfast included for all itinerary nights). Use hotel names in website copy for credibility.
