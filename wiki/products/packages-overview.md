## Standard Inclusions (all 15 packages)

1. **Private transport** — AC MPV (1–3 guests) · Toyota Hiace minibus (4–9 guests) · Hiace + MPV (10–11 guests) — fuel, tolls, parking included
2. **Dedicated crew** — driver-guide (1–3 guests) · professional driver + escort guide (4–11 guests) · licensed local site guides at key locations
3. **All entrance fees and permits** to attractions
4. **Mineral water** — daily supply
5. **Full pick-up to drop-off assistance**
6. **Complimentary JVTO travel T-shirt** (1 per participant)
7. **Meals as stated in itinerary** (minimum: breakfast all packages)

### Ijen-route additions (Ijen-relevant packages only)

- Gas masks and trekking poles for Ijen Crater hike
- **Health screening coordination** (where BBKSDA access rules require it — conditional per [[destinations/kawah-ijen]])

### Bromo-route additions (Bromo-inclusive packages only)

- Private 4WD Jeep for Mount Bromo crater area — max ≈4 guests per jeep; additional jeeps for larger groups

### Bali-origin addition (Bali packages only)

- Ferry crossing — Bali to Java (Gilimanuk–Ketapang) included

### Madakaripura caveat (CRITICAL — per SSOT §6_4)

> Helmets at Madakaripura Waterfall are **not a JVTO inclusion** — they are provided by the local waterfall site management at the canyon entrance. JVTO guides accompany guests through the approach (wading, slippery stones, narrow canyon passages). The local site team handles safety equipment on-site. **Do NOT list helmets as a JVTO-provided item for any Madakaripura route.**

## Standard Exclusions (all 15 packages)

- International / domestic flights
- Tips for drivers and guides (at guest discretion)
- Personal expenses — snacks, souvenirs, laundry (recommend ≥ IDR 500,000/person)
- Meals not stated in itinerary
- Travel insurance (optional but recommended)
- Indonesian VISA (if applicable)

## Vehicle & Crew Allocation (per SSOT §7_9)

| Group size | Vehicle | Crew |
|---|---|---|
| 1–3 guests | 1 × MPV (e.g., Toyota Avanza) | 1 × driver-guide (English-speaking) + licensed local site guides |
| 4–9 guests | 1 × Toyota Hiace (or similar 16-seat minibus) | 1 × professional driver + 1 × escort guide + licensed local site guides |
| 10–11 guests | 1 × Toyota Hiace + 1 × MPV (seating and luggage) | Additional driver for second vehicle; escort guide travels with main group |

**Bromo 4WD Jeep**: max ≈4 guests per jeep for comfort and safety; additional jeeps arranged for larger groups.

## Booking Flow (6 steps)

1. **Inquiry via WhatsApp** — message +6282244788833 with dates, group size, preferred package
2. **Package confirmation** — confirm package slug, pick-up point, special requirements
3. **Price quotation** — confirmed per pricing table for your group size
4. **Deposit payment** — 20% of total, card only via JVTO secure checkout
5. **Voucher issuance** — e-voucher issued with full trip details; pre-trip guide shared
6. **Tour execution** — private pick-up at confirmed time; full itinerary per voucher

## Payment Rules

- **Standard deposit**: 20% of total booking value (card via secure JVTO link)
- **Close-departure rule**: if Day 1 is within 14 days, JVTO may require up to 100% full payment
- **Card balance deadline**: no later than 5 days before Day 1
- **Bank wire / Wise balance deadline**: no later than 3 days before Day 1
- **Cash at JVTO office**: only if approved in writing in advance

## Cancellation Policy

| Window | Outcome |
|---|---|
| **≥ 48h before Day 1** | 100% of eligible booking value → Lifetime Travel Credit (no expiry, transferable, may be gifted/sold) |
| **< 48h before Day 1** | Fully forfeited. No Travel Credit. |

**Cash refund is not available for guest-initiated cancellation.** The Travel Credit mechanism replaces it.

**Closure policy**: If an official closure or route restriction affects the trip, JVTO offers a safe alternative route, reschedule option, or destination-fee adjustment per the confirmed booking context. See [[sources/ssot-v6]] §7_3 weather closures + [[destinations/mount-bromo]] Plan-B framework.

## Rescheduling

- **≥ 48h before Day 1**: one reschedule for the same package, subject to availability and seasonal/supplier cost difference
- **< 48h before Day 1**: handled case-by-case — may be treated as cancellation depending on committed supplier costs

## Free-of-Charge (FOC) Group Incentive

JVTO **direct bookings only** (website + WhatsApp). Not applicable to GetYourGuide, Booking.com, or other third-party platforms.

| Paying pax | FOC seats | Additional benefit |
|---|---|---|
| 18 pax | 1 FOC | — |
| 35 pax | 2 FOC | — |
| 50 pax | 3 FOC | + 5% discount for entire group |

Rules:
- "Pax" = paying guests only. FOC slot(s) do not count toward paying threshold.
- 5% group discount at the 50+ tier applies to the total package price for all paying guests.
- FOC applies to same package, same dates as the group booking.
- Must be agreed in writing during booking confirmation.
- Direct bookings only (website + WhatsApp). Not applicable to GetYourGuide, Booking.com, or other third-party platforms.

## Anti-Fraud Policy

- Use only official JVTO website, WhatsApp, email, payment links, and listed bank accounts (see [[overview]] §contact)
- JVTO **never** asks for full card numbers, CVV codes, online banking passwords, or OTP codes via chat or email
- Pause and verify through official WhatsApp or email before paying if any account number, link, or contact looks different

## Student Pricing (ISIC)

JVTO is an official ISIC provider (ID 259268 — verifiable at https://www.isic.org/discounts/?providerId=259268). Verified international students with a valid ISIC card receive student pricing. See [[credentials/trust-signals]].
