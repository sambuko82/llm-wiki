# Mount Bromo

## Entity Summary (40-word, SSOT §9_4)

Mount Bromo is an active volcano in the Tengger caldera, East Java. Best known for the Penanjakan sunrise viewpoint over the sea-of-sand caldera floor and the active smoking crater. Accessed by 4WD jeep from Cemoro Lawang in the pre-dawn window (03:00–05:00).

## Why Visitors Come

**Penanjakan (2,770 m) sunrise viewpoint** — one of Southeast Asia's most photographed sunrises. Active volcano with visible smoke plume across the vast Tengger caldera.

**Sea of Sand (Pasir Berbisik)** — ~10 km² of volcanic sand at the caldera floor. The 4WD jeep traverse is part of the experience, not optional logistics.

**Crater walk** — short walk or horse ride from the jeep drop-off to the crater rim overlooking the active smoking vent.
