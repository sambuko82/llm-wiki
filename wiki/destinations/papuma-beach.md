# Papuma Beach

## Entity Summary (40-word, SSOT §9_4)

Papuma Beach in Jember offers white sand and dramatic coastal rock formations. JVTO includes it as a coastal contrast stop on Papuma-family packages (4D+). Best for guests who want a beach break on an otherwise volcano-focused East Java itinerary. No health screening required.

## Why It's Included

Papuma is the **only coastal destination** on JVTO's East Java circuits. It functions as a deliberate contrast point between volcanic high-elevation hikes (Ijen 2,386 m night hike → Bromo 2,329 m sunrise) and the canyon waterfall stops (Tumpak Sewu). The break shifts pace, scenery, and physicality.

Two features:

1. **The beach** — white sand, calm during dry season, dramatic offshore rock formations
2. **The cape (Tanjung)** — short headland walk rising to ~86 m above sea level, offering elevated views of the coast and rocks below
