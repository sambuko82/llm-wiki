## Why a Named Crew Matters (Trust Function)

JVTO crew are individually named, photographed, and license-linked. Anonymous or interchangeable freelance crews cannot be verified. JVTO's crew are traceable via:

- KTA cards (Kartu Tanda Anggota — HPWKI member card)
- Personal social accounts (Instagram, Facebook)
- Direct guest review mentions by name (see [[reviews/trustpilot-compilation]])
- Forensic KTA card image assets

Per SSOT §4_3, this is enforced by INDECON membership: "INDECON listing explicitly validates JVTO's Local Boys employment policy — crew are recruited from local Bondowoso/Banyuwangi communities, not sourced from freelance marketplaces." Verifiable at https://www.indecon.id/spotlight-networks/java-volcano-tour-operator.
