# Permit Requirements for East Java Volcano Tours

*What licenses and permits apply to organized volcano tours in East Java? This page covers operator requirements (what JVTO holds) and visitor requirements (what guests need).*

---

## Operator Requirements

Tour operators running trips in East Java's conservation areas must hold:

| Permit | What It Is | JVTO Status |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **NIB** (Nomor Induk Berusaha) | National business registration via OSS (Online Single Submission) | ✅ 1102230032918 |
| **TDUP** (Tanda Daftar Usaha Pariwisata) | Tourism business registration with Dinas Pariwisata | ✅ Issued 2023-02-11 |
| **KBLI 79121 + 79911** | Business activity codes for Travel Agency + Tour Operator | ✅ Both registered |
| **BBKSDA operator clearance** | Authorization to operate in national parks/conservation areas | ✅ Ijen + Bromo Tengger Semeru |
| **HPWKI membership** | Ijen specialist guide association — requires BBKSDA-supervised volcanic safety training (SAR + PPGD) | ✅ AHU-0001072.AH.01.07.TAHUN 2024 |

Most operators in East Java lack one or more of these. JVTO publishes SHA-256 hashes of its credential documents in `public/llms.txt` for forensic verification. → [[credentials/legal-licenses]]

## Visitor Entry Requirements

### Kawah Ijen

| Requirement | Detail | Cost | Handled by JVTO? |
|-------------|--------|------|-------------------|
| Park entrance ticket | Required for all visitors — purchased at Paltuding gate | IDR 75,000/person (foreign tourist) | ✅ Included |
| Health certificate (surat sehat) | Required when BBKSDA SE.1658/KSA.9/2024 thresholds apply. QR-verified at gate. | IDR 35,000/person (medical checkup) | ✅ Coordinated by JVTO |
| Local guide | Required by park management for crater zone access | IDR 250,000/unit | ✅ Included |
| Gas mask | Required for crater descent/sulfur area | IDR 55,000/person | ✅ Included |
| Ijen certificate (optional) | Completion certificate available at Paltuding | IDR 35,000/person | ✅ Included |

## Why This Matters for Tourists

Most tourists don't know what permits their operator needs or what entry fees are included. Key takeaways:

1. **All entrance fees are included** in JVTO packages — no surprise costs at the gate
2. **BBKSDA operator clearance** is not universal — ask any operator to show theirs
3. **Ijen health screening** is regulatory (BBKSDA), not operator theater — JVTO coordinates it, doesn't impose it
4. **HPWKI guide certification** means government-supervised SAR + emergency medical training — not a printable diploma
