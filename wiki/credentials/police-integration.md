## What "Police-Led" Means Operationally

JVTO founder Agung Sambuko (Mr. Sam) is an **active serving officer** of the Indonesian National Police (Polri), assigned to the **Ditpamobvit** unit (Direktorat Pengamanan Objek Vital — Directorate of Vital Object Security, East Java). Ditpamobvit's mandate covers tourist safety at designated vital objects — Kawah Ijen is within this jurisdiction.

This is not a retired officer's background or an advisory role. It is active duty, documented annually in police travel orders (SPRIN) that authorize him to accompany tourist groups on field operations.

**What this means for guests**:
- Every JVTO tour operates under the oversight of someone with active institutional responsibility for tourist safety in this area — not a personal commitment but a legal duty
- Traffic Police escort for airport/ferry transfers is arranged through JVTO's formal police channels (not a commercial service)
- Police support vehicle presence at tour departures is documented — see field operations photos below

## Traffic Police Escort — How It Works

> JVTO submits a formal request to the Traffic Police (Lalu Lintas) — it does not provide its own escort or operate a private security service.

The escort is arranged through official police channels. When granted, a Traffic Police officer accompanies the transfer vehicle. This is why the escort photos show a uniformed Traffic Police officer and Polri patrol car — it is a real institutional service, not branded security guards.
