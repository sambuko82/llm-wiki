# Policy 1 — JVTO Booking, Payment & Cancellation Policy
# JVTO Booking, Payment & Cancellation Policy
Policy Route: `/policy/booking-payment-cancellation/`

### 1) Operator Identity & Official Channels

This policy applies to all enquiries, quotations, bookings, amendments, cancellations, and tour participation with Java Volcano Tour Operator (JVTO), operated by **PT Java Volcano Rendezvous**.

**Official channels (valid booking, support, and payment verification):**

- Website & secure checkout: `https://javavolcano-touroperator.com`
- WhatsApp: `+62 822-4478-8833`
- Email: `hello@javavolcano-touroperator.com`

JVTO is not responsible for any offers, instructions, or payment requests made outside these official channels.

---

### 2) Order of Precedence (If There Is Any Discrepancy)

If there is any discrepancy, the following order applies:

1. The specific terms on your **Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)** for the confirmed booking
2. This **Booking, Payment & Cancellation Policy**
3. The **Inclusions & Exclusions Policy**
4. The **Privacy & Data Protection Policy**
5. The **Travel Guide — Booking Information** (/travel-guide/booking-information)

---

### 3) Nature of JVTO Products

- JVTO operates **private, all-inclusive tours**.
- JVTO does not operate open join-in group tours and does not sell standalone “transport-only” services as its core product.
- Only inclusions explicitly stated on the official tour page and/or your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) are contractually binding.

---

### 4) When a Booking Is Confirmed

A booking is confirmed **only** when all of the following are met:

1. You select a specific official JVTO package; and
2. Your required payment (deposit or full payment) is successfully processed via approved methods; and
3. JVTO issues your **Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)** confirming the booking details.

Enquiries, messages, and quotations are not binding until the booking is confirmed under the rule above.

---

### 5) Pricing & Currency

- Official pricing and accounting are in **Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)**.

---

### 6) Payments

#### 6.1 Deposit Requirement

- Standard deposit: **20%** of the total booking value.
- If Day 1 is within **14 days**, JVTO may require up to **100%** full payment.

#### 6.2 Approved Payment Methods

**Deposit (initial payment):**

- Card payment via JVTO secure checkout (credit/debit).

**Balance payment (if applicable):**

- Card payment via secure checkout (deadline below), or
- Bank transfer / Wise (deadline below), or
- Cash at JVTO office (only if approved in writing).

#### 6.3 Balance Payment Deadlines (If Applicable)

- By card: no later than **5 days before Day 1**
- By bank transfer / Wise: no later than **3 days before Day 1**

---

### 7) Amendments & Rescheduling

#### 7.1 Amendments

If you need to correct booking details (e.g., passenger name spelling, pickup details, rooming requests), notify JVTO as soon as possible. Some changes may be subject to supplier rules, availability, and cost differences.

#### 7.2 Rescheduling (Date Change)

- Requests made **48 hours or more before Day 1**: one reschedule for the same package may be granted without fee, subject to availability and any seasonal/cost differences.
- Requests made **less than 48 hours before Day 1**: generally not permitted and may be treated as a late cancellation.

---

### 8) Cancellation Policy (48-Hour Cut-Off)

#### Definitions

- **Day 1**: the first day of services for your tour.
- Cut-off time is measured in **local Indonesia time**.

#### 8.1 Guest-Initiated Cancellation (48 Hours or More Before Day 1)

- Payments are **not refundable in cash**.
- **100% converts to JVTO Travel Credit**.

**Travel Credit rules:**

- Issued in **IDR**
- **Non-expiring**
- **Transferable** with written confirmation
- Can be used for future tours at prevailing rates

#### 8.2 Guest-Initiated Cancellation (Less Than 48 Hours Before Day 1)

- The booking may be forfeited up to **100%**.
- Travel Credit and cash refunds are generally **not provided**.

---

### 9) Force Majeure & External Events

JVTO may partially or fully cancel, postpone, or reroute a tour due to events beyond reasonable control, including (but not limited to):

- Volcanic activity, toxic gas, earthquakes, landslides, floods
- Government or park closures or restrictions
- Strikes, civil unrest, security threats
- Major infrastructure or transportation disruptions
- Severe weather or conditions rendering services unsafe

#### Compensation Principles (JVTO-Initiated)

Where JVTO cancels or significantly alters services not due to guest breach, non-payment, or no-show, JVTO may offer one or more of the following (considering irrecoverable third-party costs):

- Reasonable alternative arrangements of comparable value
- JVTO Travel Credit covering the unused portion
- A partial or full cash refund, net of non-recoverable expenses, as agreed in writing

Guest-initiated cancellations remain governed by Section 8.

---

### 10) Health, Fitness & Eligibility

- Guests are responsible for ensuring they are medically and physically fit for the itinerary.
- Certain activities (e.g., volcano hikes, night trekking, sulfur gas exposure) may be unsuitable for specific conditions.
- Where mandatory health screening or permit conditions are imposed by authorities (e.g., Mount Ijen):

  - Guests must comply with the process arranged or recognised by JVTO
  - If a guest is denied clearance, affected components are **non-refundable**, as related costs are pre-committed

- JVTO strongly recommends comprehensive travel insurance including medical cover and trip interruption.

---

### 11) Guest Responsibilities

By confirming a booking with JVTO, the lead guest and all participants agree to:

- Provide accurate and complete information during enquiry and booking
- Make payments through official channels and within deadlines
- Review the Official E-Voucher promptly and report discrepancies
- Submit pickup/logistical details within requested timeframes
- Be present on time at the agreed pickup point
- Follow safety instructions from JVTO crew and licensed guides
- Respect local laws, cultures, communities, and environments
- Hold appropriate personal travel insurance

Failure to meet these responsibilities may limit JVTO’s ability to deliver services and may trigger consequences set out in this policy.

---

# Policy 2 — JVTO Inclusions & Exclusions Policy
# JVTO Inclusions & Exclusions Policy
Policy Route: `/policy/inclusions-exclusions/`

### 1) Purpose

This policy defines what is included and not included in JVTO private tour packages. Only inclusions written on your specific **Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)** are contractually binding.

---

### 2) Contractual Rule (Write-it-to-Bind-it)

In this policy, “Official Tour Voucher” refers to your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) issued by JVTO after successful payment.

Only inclusions that appear on:

- the official JVTO package page for your chosen tour; and/or
- your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)

are legally binding components of your package.

If it is not written in your confirmed inclusions and voucher, it is not part of the contracted package, even if discussed informally.

---

### 3) Standard Inclusions (All Eligible JVTO Packages)

Unless stated otherwise on your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF), a confirmed JVTO private tour package typically includes:

#### 3.1 Transportation

- Private air-conditioned vehicle for all land sectors listed in your confirmed itinerary
- Fuel, tolls, and standard parking fees as per itinerary
- Vehicle allocation by group size (comfort and safety):

  - 2–3 guests: 1 × MPV (e.g., Toyota Avanza or similar)
  - 4–9 guests: 1 × Toyota Hiace (or similar 16-seat minibus)
  - 10–11 guests: 1 × Toyota Hiace + 1 × MPV (for seating and luggage space)

- 4WD Jeep at Mount Bromo (when Bromo is included):

  - Private jeep(s) for sunrise and crater viewpoints
  - Max. ±4 guests per jeep for comfort and safety

#### 3.2 Accommodation

- Hotel / homestay accommodation for all overnight stays listed in your itinerary
- Standard allocation (unless otherwise stated on your voucher):

  - Even-numbered groups: 1 room per 2 guests (King or Twin)
  - Odd-numbered groups: 1 room per 2 guests + 1 extra bed in one room

- Rooming principles:

  - Maximum 2 guests per standard room (excluding the designated extra bed where applicable)
  - King beds for couples or guests preferring more space
  - Twin beds for guests sharing a room

- Upgrade option (subject to request and availability): replacing an extra bed with an additional private room may be available at a published supplement (as per voucher)

#### 3.3 Professional Crew

- Licensed, vetted crew adjusted by group size and route:

  - 2–3 guests: 1 × driver-guide (English-speaking) + licensed local site guides where required
  - 4–11 guests: 1 × professional driver + 1 × escort guide + licensed local site guides at key locations
  - Additional drivers allocated when extra vehicles are used

- Crew duties include: safe driving, time management, coordination with hotels/jeeps/ferries/park authorities, and basic route explanations and guest support

#### 3.4 Tickets, Permits & Local Logistics

All listed entrance tickets and environmental/park permits connected to your confirmed itinerary, where applicable, such as:

- Mount Bromo National Park access
- Mount Ijen (including regular entrance and applicable summit/crater access where legally permitted)
- Madakaripura Waterfall
- Tumpak Sewu Waterfall
- Other named attractions in your confirmed program
- Local retribution / standard local fees where explicitly built into the package

#### 3.5 Mount Ijen Safety & Trekking Support (When Ijen is Included)

- Professional gas mask (rated for sulfur environment) for each participating guest
- Trekking poles (shared or individual, depending on configuration)
- Mandatory Ijen medical check & digital/printed clearance arranged by JVTO, conducted by licensed medical personnel at an approved facility or at your hotel in the Bondowoso area (included in the package for itineraries that visit Ijen)

#### 3.6 Meals & Water

- Daily bottled mineral water in the vehicle for overland sectors
- Hotel breakfasts for each included overnight stay
- Any additional included meals (e.g., set dinners or lunches) are included only when explicitly stated on the official package page and/or your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)

#### 3.7 Java-Bali Ferry (Where Applicable)

Ferry tickets for Java-Bali or Bali-Java sectors when:

- the crossing is listed in your itinerary; and
- the ticket is specified as included on your voucher

#### 3.8 JVTO Branded Inclusions & Group Extras

- 1 × JVTO travel T-shirt per participant for eligible packages (standard JVTO design; size selection via “Complete Your Details”)
- Custom JVTO Group T-shirt (where explicitly included): for groups of ≥12 guests in a single booking, if written on your package/voucher; 1 × custom design T-shirt per person; sizes and final design must be confirmed before production

#### 3.9 On-Trip Support & Coordination

- Pre-trip assistance via official WhatsApp/email
- Clear pickup instructions and emergency contact numbers on your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)
- On-trip coordination for timing, weather/closure adjustments, and safety decisions
- Support in case of incident (basic first response, coordination with local clinics/hospitals, and logistics adjustments where feasible)

---

### 4) “We Offer More” — Conditional Inclusions (Only If Written)

The following are included **only** when clearly stated on both:

- the official package description you booked; and
- your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF)

Conditional inclusions may include:

- Custom JVTO group T-shirts (design, quantity, eligibility conditions)
- Additional hosted meals (e.g., dinner in Bondowoso before Ijen; lunch after Tumpak Sewu; special farewell dinner)
- Bali hotel/airport transfers beyond the standard ferry crossing
- Early check-in / late check-out at hotels
- Dedicated photographer/videographer services
- Police escort or special convoy arrangements for VIP/large groups (subject to availability and regulation)

**Extra meals — specific itinerary conditions (only if written):**

- For Ijen + Bondowoso overnight itineraries:

  - Dinner at the Bondowoso hotel before the Ijen night trek
  - Lunch at the Bondowoso hotel after returning from Ijen

- For itineraries including Tumpak Sewu:

  - One additional lunch at/near Yanto Homestay (or equivalent local partner) after the Tumpak Sewu program

---

### 5) Exclusions (Simple Principle)

To keep things clear: anything that is not explicitly listed as an inclusion on the official package page and on your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) is excluded.

Common examples of not included items (unless clearly written as included for your specific booking):

- International / domestic flights and entry visas
- Travel insurance of any kind
- Meals not specified (including alcohol)
- Tips/gratuities for guides and drivers

---

### 6) Discrepancies

This policy is designed to integrate with your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF). In the event of any discrepancy, the version referenced on your booking confirmation and voucher will apply.

# Policy 3 — JVTO Privacy & Data Protection Policy
# JVTO Privacy & Data Protection Policy
Policy Route: `/policy/privacy`

### 1) Purpose

This policy explains what personal data JVTO collects, why it is collected, how it is used, and when it may be shared to operate your tour booking and comply with applicable requirements.

---

### 2) What Data We Collect

JVTO may collect the following categories of data, depending on your booking and itinerary requirements:

**2.1 Contact and booking details (lead guest):**

- Full name
- Email address
- WhatsApp/mobile number
- Booking references and tour details needed to deliver services

**2.2 Participant details (where required):**

- Guest list for accommodation and permits
- Passport/ID details and date of birth where required for permits, tickets, or legal compliance
- Pickup/drop-off details, rooming preferences, and operational notes required to deliver services

**2.3 Safety and logistics information (where relevant):**

- Dietary needs and relevant health information provided by you for safety planning and operational coordination (only where necessary)

---

### 3) Why We Collect and Use Your Data

JVTO uses personal data to:

- Issue and deliver your Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) and manage your booking
- Coordinate logistics before and during your tour (pickup timing, accommodation, vehicles, guides, route planning)
- Provide customer support and operational assistance
- Meet legal, permit, ticketing, and accommodation registration requirements where applicable
- Support safety processes and emergency response coordination where needed

---

### 4) When We Share Your Data

JVTO shares personal data only when required to deliver the booked services or comply with applicable requirements. This may include sharing necessary details with:

- Accommodation partners (for check-in/registration and rooming)
- Drivers, escort guides, local site guides, and operational teams (for coordination and guest support)
- Ticketing/permit partners and local authorities where permits or entry requirements apply
- Medical screening providers or approved facilities where mandatory screening applies (e.g., where required by authorities for specific sites)

JVTO does not share data beyond what is necessary for the specific operational purpose.

---

### 5) Payment Data

- Card payments are processed through a secure checkout and authorised payment gateway.
- JVTO does not store full card numbers, CVV codes, or online banking passwords.

---

### 6) Data Protection and Access

JVTO applies internal controls to protect personal data and limits access to authorised personnel and partners only when needed for service delivery.
