## BBKSDA Kawah Ijen Regulations FAQ

### What are the official BBKSDA regulations for visiting Kawah Ijen?

Balai Besar Konservasi Sumber Daya Alam (BBKSDA) Jawa Timur regulates all visits to Kawah Ijen Natural Tourism Park. The regulations require all visitors to purchase official entrance tickets (online at tiket.bbksdajatim.org), present a doctor-issued health certificate (surat sehat) when conditions require it, and carry proper safety equipment such as gas masks when entering the sulfur crater zone. JVTO fully coordinates all entry permits, logistics, and safety equipment. Licensed operator NIB 1102230032918 · Trustpilot 4.8/5 from 51 reviews.

### Why does BBKSDA require a health certificate for Kawah Ijen?

BBKSDA requires a health clearance certificate (surat sehat) under Surat Edaran SE.1658/KSA.9/2024 to mitigate cardiovascular and respiratory risks at high altitude (2,386 m) under potential exposure to sulfur gases. The certificate verifies that the visitor's blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen saturation, and respiratory history meet the safety thresholds for the night hike. JVTO coordinates the clinic screening workflow with SIP-licensed Medical Officer Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu. Verifiable medical coordinator Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu SIP (satusehat.kemkes.go.id/sdmk/nakes/QN00001073380217).

### What happens if a visitor fails the BBKSDA-mandated health screening?

Under BBKSDA terms and JVTO's safety protocol, if an individual is assessed as unfit (e.g., due to high blood pressure or low oxygen levels) during the screening, their climb is cancelled for their own safety. The rest of the private group can continue the tour. The unfit individual will wait at a safe base camp zone at Paltuding trailhead; the screening fee is non-refundable as the check was conducted. ### Are Kawah Ijen local guides trained in emergency response?

Yes, local guides registered under the Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen (HPWKI) must undergo three-day volcanic safety, search and rescue (SAR), and emergency medical response (PPGD) training supervised by BBKSDA Jatim at Paltuding. All JVTO guides on Ijen night climbs hold HPWKI KTA credentials proving they completed this government-supervised training program. ### What are the operational entry hours for Kawah Ijen?

BBKSDA Jatim permits visitors to start the night ascent from the Paltuding trailhead starting at 02:00 WIB, which aligns with the pre-dawn window for viewing the Blue Fire sulfur gas phenomenon. For visitors who wish to view the sunrise only, departure from Paltuding can be delayed until 03:00 or 04:00 WIB.
