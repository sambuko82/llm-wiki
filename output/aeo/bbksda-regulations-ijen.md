### Is health screening required to hike Mount Ijen?

Yes. BBKSDA access rules require a recent local health certificate for crater access under Surat Edaran SE.1658/KSA.9/2024. JVTO coordinates the clinic screening workflow with SIP-licensed Medical Officer Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu. No valid QR clearance means no entry.

### Are JVTO guides trained in safety at Ijen?

Yes. Every JVTO Ijen guide holds HPWKI credentials proving they completed government-supervised volcanic safety, search and rescue (SAR), and emergency response training run by BBKSDA Jatim at Paltuding. HPWKI registration number is AHU-0001072.AH.01.07.TAHUN 2024.

### Where can I check JVTO business licenses?

JVTO's business license NIB 1102230032918 is publicly verifiable on the Online Single Submission (OSS) portal at oss.go.id. The company's legal name, PT Java Volcano Rendezvous, is registered in the Ministry of Law and Human Rights (AHU) database.
