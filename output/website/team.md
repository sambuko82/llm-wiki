# Meet the Team

## Hero

**H1**: The People Behind Your Tour

Every JVTO tour runs on a named crew — not anonymous freelancers pulled from a marketplace. Our 14-member field team consists of seven HPWKI-registered guides and seven dedicated drivers, all recruited from local Bondowoso and Banyuwangi communities.

Each guide holds a KTA (Kartu Tanda Anggota) credential issued by HPWKI, the state-recognized association for Ijen Crater tour operators. That KTA means they completed volcanic safety training, search-and-rescue protocols, and first-aid certification — all supervised by BBKSDA Jawa Timur (the East Java Natural Resources Conservation Agency).

When guests mention crew by name in reviews, that is the system working as designed.

## Founder

**Agung "Mr. Sam" Sambuko** — Founder & Active Tourist Police Officer

Mr. Sam is an active officer of the Indonesian Tourist Police (Ditpamobvit East Java), rank Bripka. He founded JVTO after identifying safety gaps in East Java volcano tourism: unlicensed operators, no medical screening, inconsistent guides. PT Java Volcano Rendezvous was incorporated in 2016 as the response — private-only model, verifiable credentials, police-level operational discipline.

He still deploys to Ijen overnight in 10°C weather to enforce health protocols. The safety standard is not corporate policy — it is personal practice.

## The Credential Chain

Every guide's KTA card traces back to institutional accountability:

1. **Guest meets guide** → guide carries KTA card
2. **KTA issued by HPWKI** → Himpunan Pelaku Wisata Khusus Ijen (AHU-0001072.AH.01.07.TAHUN 2024)
3. **HPWKI membership requires** → volcanic safety training, SAR protocols, first-aid certification
4. **Training supervised by** → BBKSDA Jawa Timur (documented: "Pelatihan Pemandu Kawah Ijen", 2024-05-24)

This chain is not marketing. It is an auditable institutional record. The HPWKI decree is verifiable at ahu.go.id. The BBKSDA training report is published at bbksdajatim.org.
