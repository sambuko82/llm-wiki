# Bromo & Ijen Itinerary Guide — How Many Days Do You Need?

JVTO operates 16 private tour packages across East Java. The right one depends on how many days you have, which destinations matter to you, and where you start. This guide breaks down what each duration actually covers.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Duration | What you see | Starting from (2 pax) | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1D1N | Bromo sunrise only | IDR 1,550,000 | Short on time, already near Surabaya |
| 2D1N | Bromo + Madakaripura OR Ijen only | IDR 2,300,000 | Weekend trip, single destination |
| 3D2N | Ijen + Bromo + Madakaripura | IDR 3,570,000 | Classic combination, most popular |

## 1-Day: Bromo Midnight Experience

**Package**: `bromo-1d1n` (Surabaya origin only)

Depart Surabaya late at night. Jeep to Penanjakan viewpoint for Milky Way and sunrise. Descend to Sea of Sand, walk to crater. Breakfast included. Return to Surabaya by midday.

**What you miss**: Ijen, waterfalls, the slower pace of a multi-day trip.
**Who picks this**: Business travelers with one free night, cruise ship passengers, anyone already staying in Surabaya.

---

## 2-Day: Single Destination Focus

Two options:

**Bromo + Madakaripura** (`bromo-2d1n`): Day 1 transfer to Bromo area. Day 2 sunrise + Madakaripura waterfall canyon trek + return to Surabaya. No Ijen.

**Ijen Blue Fire** (`ijen-2d1n`): Day 1 transfer to Bondowoso, dinner, health screening. Day 2 midnight Ijen climb, blue fire window, crater lake at sunrise, return to Surabaya.

**Key difference**: Bromo is a jeep-based sunrise experience. Ijen is a physical night hike with volcanic gas exposure — requires medical screening.

---

## 3-Day: The Classic Combination

The most-booked duration. Two route directions:

**Ijen → Bromo → Madakaripura** (`ijen-bromo-madakaripura-3d2n`, SUB→SUB):
- Day 1: Surabaya → Bondowoso. Health screening. Dinner.
- Day 2: Midnight Ijen climb → descent → lunch → overland transfer to Bromo area (~5 hours).
- Day 3: Bromo sunrise → Madakaripura waterfall → return to Surabaya.

**Bromo → Madakaripura → Ijen** (`bromo-madakaripura-ijen-3d2n`, SUB→Bali):
- Day 1: Surabaya → Bromo area staging.
- Day 2: Bromo sunrise → Madakaripura → transfer east to Bondowoso. Health screening.
- Day 3: Midnight Ijen climb → Ketapang ferry → finish in Bali.

**Which direction?** If you fly into Surabaya and out of Bali (or vice versa), pick the route that matches your flight sequence. The SUB→Bali direction avoids backtracking.

---

## 4-Day: Full Circuit or Relaxed Pace

Three variants:

**Ijen + Bromo + Madakaripura + Surabaya finish** (`ijen-bromo-madakaripura-4d3n`): Same as 3-day but adds a Surabaya city overnight on Day 3, with Day 4 as a clean airport transfer. Suited for late-departing flights.

**Ijen + Papuma + Tumpak Sewu + Bromo** (`ijen-papuma-tumpak-sewu-bromo-4d3n`): Adds Papuma Beach and Tumpak Sewu waterfall. The full East Java circuit in 4 days — volcanoes, beach, and waterfall canyon.

**Tumpak Sewu + Bromo + Ijen → Bali** (`tumpak-sewu-bromo-ijen-4d3n`): Starts with Tumpak Sewu, ends with Ijen crossing to Bali. Different route direction for different flight logistics.

---

## 5-Day: Comprehensive Coverage

**Full circuit** (`ijen-papuma-tumpak-sewu-bromo-5d4n`): All five destinations — Ijen, Papuma Beach, Tumpak Sewu, Bromo, plus a Surabaya finish. Extra day vs the 4-day gives more breathing room at each stop.

**Ijen + Bromo + Madakaripura + Malang** (`ijen-bromo-madakaripura-malang-5d4n`): Swaps the coastal/waterfall segment for Malang city and Batu area exploration. Day 4 shifts away from mountain rhythm — city, food, and culture.

---

## 6-Day: The Grand Tour

**Grand East Java** (`ijen-papuma-tumpak-sewu-bromo-malang-6d5n`): Everything. Ijen, Papuma, Tumpak Sewu, Bromo, Malang, and Surabaya. Six days covers every JVTO destination with room for rest between volcanic segments.

## Transfer Times Between Destinations

| Segment | Drive time |
|---|---|
| Surabaya → Bromo (Cemoro Lawang) | ~3 hours |
| Ijen (Paltuding) → Surabaya | 5–6 hours |
| Tumpak Sewu → Bromo | 3–4 hours |
| Bali → Ijen (Banyuwangi side) | 5–7 hours (includes ferry) |

All transfers are in your private vehicle with a dedicated driver. No shared transport.

---

## Recurring Patterns You'll See

**Ijen staging**: Arrive evening → health screening at hotel → midnight departure to Paltuding → 1.5–2 hour ascent → blue fire window → crater lake at sunrise → descent.

**Bromo sunrise**: 01:00–02:00 departure → jeep to Penanjakan → Milky Way window → sunrise (~05:30) → descent to Sea of Sand → crater walk → breakfast at hotel.

These sequences are fixed by natural timing (sunrise, gas conditions). The packages differ in what comes before and after.

---

## How to Choose

1. **How many days do you have?** The comparison table above narrows it immediately.
2. **Where do you fly in/out?** If you arrive Surabaya and depart Bali (or reverse), pick a cross-island route to avoid backtracking.
3. **Do you want Ijen?** 12 of 16 packages include Ijen. If you do, medical screening is mandatory and included.
4. **Beach or city?** 5-day has two variants — coastal (Papuma) or urban (Malang). Pick your preference.

All packages are private — schedule adjustments happen in real time with your crew.
