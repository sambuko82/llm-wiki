# Booking Information — Payments, Changes & Travel Credit

Everything you need to know to confirm, pay, and manage a JVTO booking. The full legal text is at [javavolcano-touroperator.com/policy/booking-payment-cancellation](https://javavolcano-touroperator.com/policy/booking-payment-cancellation). What follows is the practical summary.

---

## How to Book

Booking with JVTO is a five-step process:

1. **Choose your route and dates** — browse packages on the website or contact us on WhatsApp (+62 822 4478 8833) with your dates, origin (Surabaya or Bali), group size, and preferred route.
2. **Receive your tailored proposal** — we send a personalised itinerary and price quotation for your specific group.
3. **Confirm and pay** — you confirm; we issue a secure payment link.
4. **Receive your Official E-Voucher** — a PDF is issued after deposit payment. This is your booking reference and includes your full trip details and pre-trip guide.
5. **Access My Booking Portal** — post-payment, you gain access to your personal booking dashboard using your lead traveller email.

Booking is confirmed only when: (1) a package has been selected, (2) the required payment has been processed, and (3) the Official E-Voucher / Invoice (PDF) has been issued by JVTO.

## Payment Steps and Details

**Standard deposit**: 20% of total booking value, paid by card via the secure JVTO checkout (SSL-encrypted, PCI DSS-compliant).

**If Day 1 is within 14 days**: up to 100% full payment may be required at the time of booking.

**Balance payment deadlines**:
- By card: no later than 5 days before Day 1
- By bank wire or Wise: no later than 3 days before Day 1
- Cash at JVTO office (IDR only): only if approved in writing

**All pricing is in IDR.** Prices are quoted and invoiced in Indonesian Rupiah.

## FOC Group Discount Scheme

JVTO direct bookings (website and WhatsApp only) receive FOC (Free of Charge) incentives based on group size:

| Paying guests | FOC seats | Additional discount |
|---|---|---|
| 18+ | 1 FOC seat | — |
| 35+ | 2 FOC seats | — |
| 50+ | 3 FOC seats | **+ 5% discount on total package price for the entire group** |

FOC benefits apply to "paying guests" only and must be agreed in writing during the booking process. This scheme is not available through third-party platforms (GetYourGuide, Booking.com, etc.).

## My Booking Portal

After payment, the lead traveller gains access to My Booking Portal using the email address provided at booking. The portal is your live record of the trip and your task management tool for the pre-tour checklist.

**What the portal shows:**
- Tour dates and package summary
- Pick-up details and vehicle/crew assignment
- Accommodation plan
- Daily itinerary
- Notes from JVTO
- E-Voucher download link

**What the portal lets you do:**
- Submit your guest list
- Confirm pick-up and drop-off addresses
- Set rooming preferences
- Note dietary requirements
- Complete your pre-trip to-do checklist

Access My Booking Portal at [javavolcano-touroperator.com](https://javavolcano-touroperator.com) using your lead traveller email.

## Your E-Voucher — The Booking Reference

The Official E-Voucher (PDF) is issued after deposit payment. It is your primary booking reference. Keep it accessible throughout your trip — digital on your phone is sufficient for most checkpoints; a paper passport may be required at crater-access gates.

The E-Voucher shows: tour dates, package inclusions, pick-up time and location, contact details for WhatsApp and email, and payment status.

In all disputes, the E-Voucher takes precedence over verbal confirmations or informal messages. Check it when it arrives and flag any discrepancies to JVTO immediately.

## Amendments and Reschedules

One free reschedule is available if requested 48 hours or more before Day 1, subject to availability. Reschedules requested less than 48 hours before Day 1 are not permitted.

Significant route changes that alter the primary destination sequence are not available — JVTO does not offer unbundled or transport-only services.

---

## Cancellation and Travel Credit

For guest-initiated cancellations:
- **≥ 48 hours before Day 1**: 100% converts to Lifetime Travel Credit — non-expiring, transferable with written authorisation.
- **< 48 hours before Day 1**: booking forfeited up to 100%; no Travel Credit is issued.

Cash refunds are not available for guest-initiated cancellations. For closures caused by volcanic activity, government restrictions, or force majeure, JVTO may offer Travel Credit, alternatives, or partial cash refunds — assessed per situation.
