## Taiwan Logistics Answers

### Section Heading
Questions Taiwanese travelers ask before booking

**Do I need Mandarin support?**
JVTO's standard operating language for international trips is English. For Taiwanese travelers, the booking handoff can be kept simple: route name, date, pax count, pickup point, passport names for permits, and final payment deadline. Phase 2 of this page should add Traditional Chinese booking microcopy after translation QA.

**Should I start from Bali or Surabaya?**
Start from Bali if your easiest international flight is TPE→DPS or if Bali is already part of your holiday. Start from Surabaya if you want the shortest route to Bromo and a cleaner East Java-only itinerary.

**Can I book on Klook and still be supported by JVTO?**
Yes — if your voucher names JVTO / Java Volcano Tour Operator as the operator, JVTO services the operational side. For date changes, refunds, and OTA-specific rules, follow the Klook channel instructions because OTA policy may control the transaction. For direct JVTO bookings, JVTO's direct policy applies.

**Can I pay in TWD?**
JVTO's official pricing and accounting are in **IDR**. Card providers, Wise, banks, Klook, or other OTAs may display or settle in another currency, but JVTO's direct quote is IDR-based.

**Is the 20% deposit enough?**
For direct bookings, the standard deposit is 20% of total booking value. If Day 1 is within 14 days, JVTO may require up to full payment.

**When is the balance due?**
For direct bookings: card balance no later than 5 days before Day 1; bank transfer or Wise no later than 3 days before Day 1.

**What visa should Taiwan passport holders check?**
Check Indonesia Immigration's official e-VOA / eVisa portal before travel. Entry rules can change; do not rely on social media screenshots or third-party visa agents without verifying against the official Indonesian immigration domain.

## FAQ Section

### Can Taiwanese travelers do Bromo and Ijen in 3 days?
Yes. The standard fast routes are 3D2N from Surabaya or Bali. Choose Surabaya return for an East Java loop, Bali return if your trip is based in Bali, or Bali→Surabaya if you want one-way overland travel.

### Which route includes Papuma and Tumpak Sewu?
Choose the 4D3N or 5D4N Ijen + Papuma + Tumpak Sewu + Bromo circuit. It exists from Surabaya return, Bali→Surabaya, and Surabaya→Bali route logic.

### Is Ijen Blue Fire guaranteed?
No. Blue Fire is a natural phenomenon and depends on weather, gas activity, access rules, and safety decisions at the time of the hike.

### Is Tumpak Sewu suitable for everyone?
No. Tumpak Sewu involves steep, wet, and uneven terrain. Travelers with knee, balance, or fitness concerns should ask JVTO before choosing the full waterfall descent.

### Can JVTO pick us up at Bali airport?
For Bali-origin routes, pickup can be arranged from Bali hotel or address according to the package handoff. Confirm airport pickup timing before booking because ferry crossing and long-distance driving require realistic buffers.

### Can we end in Surabaya and fly home to Taiwan the same day?
Do not book a tight same-day international connection after Bromo sunrise. Use a late flight only if JVTO confirms the timing is realistic, or stay one night in Surabaya.

### Are tours private or shared?
JVTO tours are private. Your group has its own vehicle and operating crew; JVTO does not run open join-in group tours as its core product.

### Can we use LINE?
The official published chat channel is WhatsApp. If another channel is used operationally, confirm it through WhatsApp or email first.
