## Address Continuity — The Institutional Anchor

The Booking.com 2015 award was shipped to:

> "Agung, Jl. Khairil Anwar No.102, Bondowoso"

PT Java Volcano Rendezvous is registered and operates today at:

> Jl. Khairil Anwar No.102 A, Badean, Bondowoso, Jawa Timur 68214

Same street, same number — suffix "A" reflecting standard Indonesian address subdivision. Google Maps CID: 1266403973589689021 → https://www.google.com/maps?cid=1266403973589689021

The address appears on the 2015 Booking.com shipping record, the 2016 PT AHU registration, and the current operational website. This is the strongest form of continuity evidence: an independent logistics record (a physical award delivery) predating the PT entity by the same address line.

## What the Record Proves

The three independent record types — a logistics artifact (Booking.com award shipping), an AHU corporate registry entry, and a third-party guidebook citation — each resolve to the same operator at the same address across an 11-year period (2015 → 2026).

No gap in the record is self-reported. Each proof layer is independently issued:

- Booking.com: issued by Booking.com based on guest review data
- AHU: issued by the Indonesian Ministry of Law and Human Rights
- Stefan Loose: published by an independent European travel publisher
- TDUP: issued by Dinas Pariwisata via OSS

Same operator. Same location. Documented from 2015 guesthouse era through 2016 PT incorporation and 2023 TDUP formalization.
