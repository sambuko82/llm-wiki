## What All Tours Share

Every JVTO tour — regardless of origin, duration, or destinations — comes with the same operational baseline:

- **100% private** — dedicated vehicle, driver, and guide from pick-up to drop-off. No shared groups, ever.
- **All-inclusive** — private transport (fuel, tolls, parking), accommodation + breakfast, entrance fees, mineral water, and 1 JVTO travel T-shirt per participant. No hidden costs.
- **Police-led operator** — JVTO is founded and led by Mr. Sam, an active Tourist Police officer. Licensed operator NIB 1102230032918. Licenses verifiable: NIB, TDUP, HPWKI, POLPAR, BBKSDA, ISIC.

## Ijen Routes — Health Screening

12 of 16 JVTO packages include Kawah Ijen. Ijen access rules can require a recent local health certificate. JVTO coordinates the clinic workflow when rules apply. Gas masks are provided by JVTO on all Ijen routes.

This section is shown on individual tour pages only when the package includes Ijen.
