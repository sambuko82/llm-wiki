## Route Overview

### Section Heading
Bali to East Java — what the route looks like

All four Bali packages cross from Bali to East Java by ferry on Day 1. Three packages finish in Surabaya (one-way overland); one package returns you to Bali on Day 3. The route flows east-to-west: Ijen crater first, Bromo last (or Bromo first, Ijen last on the 3D2N return package). Pick-up is from your Bali hotel or address.

## What's Included Section

### Section Heading
Every Bali package includes — in writing

- **Bali–Java ferry crossing**: Gilimanuk–Ketapang, both directions (3D2N Bali-return package) or one-way Bali-to-Java (all other packages).
- **Private transport**: AC MPV (1–3 guests) or Toyota Hiace (4–9 guests). Fuel, tolls, and parking included.
- **Dedicated crew**: English-speaking driver-guide (1–3 guests), or professional driver + escort guide (4+ guests). Licensed local site guides at key locations.
- **Entrance fees and permits** to all attractions on the itinerary.
- **Accommodation + breakfast** (all nights, per itinerary).
- **Private 4WD Jeep** for Bromo crater area. One jeep per ≤4 guests; additional jeeps arranged for larger groups.
- **Gas masks and trekking poles** for Ijen crater hike.
- **Health-certificate screening coordination** for Ijen routes when current access rules require it.
- **Daily mineral water supply**.
- **Full pick-up to drop-off assistance** from your Bali hotel or address.
- **JVTO travel T-shirt** (1 per participant).

**Not included**: flights, tips, personal expenses, travel insurance, Indonesian VISA (if applicable).

**End point note**: Three of four Bali packages finish in Surabaya — plan onward transport from Surabaya (flights, train, or bus) in advance.
