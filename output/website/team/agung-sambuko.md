# Agung "Mr. Sam" Sambuko

**Role**: Founder & Active Tourist Police Officer
**Rank**: Bripka (Brigadir Kepala)
**Unit**: Ditpamobvit East Java (Directorate of Vital Object Security)

> "We saw the gaps in safety standards first-hand and decided to build something different: private-only routes, realistic driving days, and clear written rules."

Mr. Sam grew up in Bondowoso near Kawah Ijen. He operated the Ijen Bondowoso Homestay from 2015, earning a Booking.com Guest Review Award (9.4/10). As an active Tourist Police officer, he saw safety gaps first-hand — unlicensed operators, no medical screening, inconsistent guides. PT Java Volcano Rendezvous was incorporated in 2016 as the response.

He still deploys to Ijen overnight in 10°C weather to enforce health protocols. In March 2021, during a COVID-era long-holiday weekend, he stayed on-post at Kawah Wurung for days without going home — documented by Detik.com with his police rank cited. The safety standard is personal practice, not corporate policy.

No other East Java volcano tour operator is led by an active Tourist Police officer. His SPRIN documents are on file, his rank appears in independent press coverage, and his police unit (Ditpamobvit) makes tourist safety a duty — not a marketing position.
