# Joyo

**Role**: Driver
**KTA**: KTA-D-2024-011

Joyo is the precision professional on the JVTO driver team. His self-description is unusually specific: he names both speed and control as non-negotiable. On East Java's mountain roads — narrow, steep, shared with trucks and motorbikes — that combination matters. Fast enough to keep multi-day itineraries on schedule. Clean enough that guests sleep through transfers instead of gripping the door handle.
