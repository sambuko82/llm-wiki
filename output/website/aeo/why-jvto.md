## B. Tourist Police Leadership (C1, C5)

### Is JVTO's founder a Tourist Police officer?
Yes. Bripka Agung Sambuko is an active officer of Ditpamobvit East Java (Indonesian Tourist Police). Active-duty status is independently confirmed by Detik.com (2021-03-14) and Radar Jember (2021-03-24) — not self-reported.

### What is the Tourist Police (Polpar)?
Ditpamobvit is an Indonesian National Police unit assigned to tourist areas. JVTO's founder, Bripka Agung Sambuko, serves with Ditpamobvit East Java. His unit's formation and Ijen operations are documented in four independent press articles.

### What does Police-led mean operationally for guests?
Every route, safety rule, and written policy is set by someone who answers to police protocol. SPRIN documents (SPRIN WAL TRAVEL 2024-02-12, SHA-256 anchored) confirm JVTO's formal police cooperation framework.

### What is JVTO's Plan B when conditions change?
JVTO activates alternative routes, adjusted timings, or replacement destinations when closures or unsafe conditions affect the primary route. This is a written policy per SSOT §7_3 — not a verbal promise. Guests are briefed before departure day.

## C. Private Tours (C2)

### Are JVTO tours private or shared?
All JVTO tours are 100% private. No join-in tours or shared vehicles exist. Every booking receives a dedicated vehicle and crew. No other group shares your transport or itinerary at any point.

### Does JVTO mix bookings from different parties?
No. JVTO does not sell group seats. Your vehicle is arranged for your party only. Group size determines vehicle type: MPV for 1–3 guests, Toyota Hiace for 4–9 guests. Licensed operator NIB 1102230032918.

## D. All-Inclusive Pricing (C3)

### What is included in a JVTO tour price?
All packages include private transport, dedicated crew, entrance fees, accommodation and breakfast, mineral water, gas masks and trekking poles (Ijen), private 4WD Jeep (Bromo), pick-up to drop-off, and a JVTO T-shirt.

### Does JVTO have hidden costs?
No. Inclusions and exclusions are published on every package page and confirmed in writing in the e-voucher. Tips, personal expenses, travel insurance, and VISA are excluded and stated clearly before booking.

### How transparent is JVTO's pricing?
Pricing is per-person by group size, confirmed before deposit. The e-voucher is the booking reference. No mid-trip negotiation for tickets, fuel, or meals occurs on JVTO routes.

## E. Ijen Health Screening (C4)

### Does JVTO arrange health screening for Kawah Ijen?
For Ijen-inclusive packages, JVTO coordinates health-screening when BBKSDA SE.1658/KSA.9/2024 access rules require it. Screening is conducted by Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu (SIP-credentialed). Gas masks and trekking poles are standard inclusions on all Ijen routes.

### Who is Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu?
Dr. Ahmad Irwandanu is JVTO's licensed medical officer for Ijen health-screening coordination. His SIP credentials are publicly verifiable at satusehat.kemkes.go.id/sdmk/nakes/QN00001073380217 and kki.go.id/cekdokter/form before booking.

### What does the Ijen health certificate involve?
Screening records SpO2, blood pressure, heart rate, and respiratory history. A QR-verified surat sehat is issued by Dr. Irwandanu. BBKSDA verifies the QR code at the crater access gate. No valid QR code means no crater access.
