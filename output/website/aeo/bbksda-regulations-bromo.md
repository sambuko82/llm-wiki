### Do you need a permit to visit Mount Bromo?

Yes. Visitors must book entrance permits in advance via the official TNBTS online system. Private passenger cars are prohibited from entering the caldera floor. JVTO manages all permits and provides a private 4WD jeep for the traverse.

### What is the safety zone limit at Mount Bromo?

Mount Bromo is monitored by PVMBG and is under Level II (Waspada) status. Visitors are strictly prohibited from entering within a 1 km radius of the active crater rim. JVTO guides enforce these safety limits on all tours.

### Are Bromo jeeps private on JVTO tours?

Yes. Every Bromo package includes a private 4WD jeep (maximum 4 guests per vehicle for safety and comfort) reserved for your group. JVTO does not use shared jeeps or mixed bookings.
