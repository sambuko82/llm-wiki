### Does JVTO provide gas masks for Kawah Ijen?

Yes. JVTO provides professional chemical gas masks (half-face respirators) and trekking poles for all Kawah Ijen tours. These items are standard inclusions at no extra cost, distributed by your guide at the Paltuding trailhead.

### Why is a gas mask necessary at Mount Ijen?

A gas mask is required to protect your respiratory system from highly toxic sulfur dioxide (SO2) gas concentrated near the crater floor and the Blue Fire. Descent into the crater is prohibited by park rangers without proper safety respirators.

### What equipment is included in a JVTO Ijen tour?

Every JVTO Ijen private tour includes an AC transport vehicle, an English-speaking guide, entrance fees, the mandatory health-screening coordination, a professional half-face gas mask, a headlamp, trekking poles, bottled water, and a JVTO travel T-shirt.
